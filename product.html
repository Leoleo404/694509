About the Google Pixel Watch

After months of speculation, leaks, and a sneak peek in May, Google finally revealed its first smart watch in October 2022. Google's Pixel Watch is modern, compact, and loaded with cutting-edge software. The Google Pixel Watch now has a distinct appearance. The manufacturer chose a 1.2-inch circular amoled screen with true black. The case measures 41 millimetres in diameter, and the screen edges are quite thick in comparison to other smartwatches. Google was also able to add an always-on function using amoled techniques. You can see the time and important information without having to turn on the wearable.
